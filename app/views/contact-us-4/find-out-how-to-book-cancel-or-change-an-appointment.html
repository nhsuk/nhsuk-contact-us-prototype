Find out how to book, cancel or change an appointment

GP appointments

Manage GP appointments online

You may be able to book, cancel or change a GP appointment by logging into your NHS account on the NHS website or NHS App.

To do this, you must be:

  • registered with a GP surgery
  • aged 13 or over

When you create an account, you'll be asked to prove your identity.

Log in or create an account

You may be able to use other GP online services and apps to book, cancel or change a GP appointment. Ask your GP surgery which you can use.

Contact the GP surgery directly

You can book, cancel or change your GP appointment by contacting your GP surgery.

Find contact details for a GP surgery
Hospital appointments

Contact the hospital directly

You can book, cancel or change a hospital appointment by contacting a hospital directly by phone or through their website.
