Give feedback about an NHS service

You can give feedback by speaking to a member of staff at the NHS hospital, GP surgery, dentist, opticians or pharmacy you went to.

Or you can leave a rating or review on their profile page on nhs.uk.

Find an NHS service

Make a complaint about an NHS service

You can complain to a member of staff at the NHS service you went to, such as a GP surgery or hospital.

Or you can complain to the organisation in charge.

Contact NHS England if your complaint is about:

  • a GP surgery
  • an NHS dentist
  • an NHS optician
  • a pharmacy
  • NHS 111

Contact your local integrated care board (ICB) if your complaint is about:

  • hospital care
  • mental health services
  • out of hours services
  • community services, such as district nursing
