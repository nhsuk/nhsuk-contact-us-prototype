Find out how to leave a review of an NHS service

Leave a rating and review online

You can leave a rating and review of any NHS service on their profile page on the NHS website.

To do this:

  • find the service you want to review
  • open the service profile
  • go to the 'leave a review' page
  • use the online form to leave a rating and review of the service
Find an NHS service

More information

  • Find out more about how we manage patient feedback, including ratings and reviews
  • Watch the ratings and reviews service video on YouTube

Give feedback in person

You can give feedback by speaking to a member of staff at the NHS hospital, GP surgery, dentist, opticians or pharmacy you went to.
