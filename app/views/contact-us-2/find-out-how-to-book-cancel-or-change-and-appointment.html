GP appointments

GP appointments online

You may be able to book, cancel or change a GP appointment by logging into your NHS account on the NHS website or NHS App.

You can create an NHS account if you do not already have one. To do this, you must be:

  • registered with a GP surgery
  • aged 16 or over

When you create an account, you'll be asked to prove your identity

Access your NHS services
