Get medical help

If you think it's an emergency:

  • call 999
  • find your nearest A&E

If you need help now, but it's not an emergency:

  • go to 111.nhs.uk or call 111
  • speak to a pharmacist for advice on medicines or common problems like coughs, colds and rashes
  • See a GP or dentist

If you'd like to read more:

  • visit the nhs.uk Health A to Z to read about conditions, symptoms and treatment information
  • visit the nhs.uk Medicines A to Z to find out how your medicine works, how and when to take it, and possible side effects
