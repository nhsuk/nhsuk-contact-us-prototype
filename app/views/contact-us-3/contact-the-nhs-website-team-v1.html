Contact the NHS website team

Use this service to contact the NHS website service desk to:

  • report an issue or error on the NHS website
  • get help accessing the NHS website using assistive technology
  • ask a question about something on the NHS website
Information:

The NHS website service desk cannot help with medical questions or booking appointments with NHS healthcare services.

Do not leave any confidential or personal medical information in your message, such as your symptoms.

Find out how to contact the NHS for other reasons.

Important: To get medical help

  • Get help from NHS 111 online or call 111 if you need medical help right now.
  • Call 999 if you need medical help for a life-threatening emergency.
  • Find out where to get urgent help for mental health.
  • Contact your GP surgery for other non-urgent medical help.
